# Piera Bretton

**AI Product Manager | Product strategy, customer insight, and applied AI execution**

I work at the point where **customer problems, business strategy, and AI systems** become practical products. My focus is turning ambiguous opportunities into clear product bets: the user problem, the workflow, the data, the model behaviour, the evaluation plan, the operating risks, and the commercial outcome.

I am building this GitHub as a public product portfolio. The aim is not to present myself as a software engineer. It is to show how I think, how I structure AI product work, and how I collaborate with technical teams to move from insight to shipped outcomes.

## How I approach AI product work

| Product question | What I make visible |
|---|---|
| **Why this problem?** | Customer pain, market context, business value, and the strategic reason to act now. |
| **What should be built?** | PRDs, user journeys, opportunity sizing, success metrics, and launch scope. |
| **How should AI be used?** | LLM/agent workflow, data requirements, evaluation approach, guardrails, and human-in-the-loop design. |
| **How do we know it works?** | Product analytics, experiment design, quality measures, adoption metrics, and feedback loops. |
| **How do we launch responsibly?** | Risk register, failure modes, model limitations, compliance considerations, and post-launch monitoring. |

## What I want this profile to signal

A strong AI PM should be able to do more than talk about AI. They should be able to frame a problem clearly, make trade-offs explicit, understand model and data constraints, define quality, manage risk, and connect product decisions to measurable outcomes. This profile is my working record of that craft.
